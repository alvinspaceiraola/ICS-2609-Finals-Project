Best Time to Go

The best time to visit Seoul is from March to May, and from September to November, when the weather is mild, and travel expenses are low.


Weather

Springtime happens during March until May, it has a high temperature of 50-75 degrees, but low during the evenings. Rainy Season happens around June until August with oppressive humidity, long lines, torrential downpours and high travel costs. From September to November, Seoul experiences pleasant weather in fall: Daily highs stay in the mid-50s and mid-70s. From December to February, temperatures hover between the 20s and 30s.


Getting Around

  • KTX

    • The largest railway operator is Korail. KTS is the express railway service in Seoul that can take you to different places like Busan, Gyengju, Mokpo, and more. A KR pass is available for foreigners who wants to avail and its price varies depending on how many days and age group ranging from ₩61,000 to ₩210,000.

  • Seoul Subway

    • Seoul’s modern and efficient public transportation which includes both subway trains and buses. A train comes about every 5 minutes and are clean. For convenience, it is easy to use since signs are both in Korean and English. Fares cost between ₩1,250 and ₩2,000

  • T-Card

    • The cheapest way to use public transport is to use a T card, which is an integrated fare system which is transferable from bus to bus, bus to subway, or vice-versa. The T- card can be either prepaid or postpaid (through a credit card). The prepaid card is 2,500 won. They can be purchased from the automated machines in every subway station or convenience store. T- cards can be recharged for amounts from 1,000 won to 90,000 won.


Popular Attractions in Seoul


Gyeongbokgung Palace

Gyeongbokgung Palace is a large complex that houses the National Folk Museum in Korea. Restaurants, cafés, and art museum can be found nearby the place. An individual can get a free admission when wearing a hanbok dress which can be rented around the town.


N Seoul Tower

N Seoul Tower is Seoul's second highest point which is located a Namsan Mountain. It is a common place for couples to leave their love locks up in the tower. There are different ways to go there: Ride the Namsan Shuttle Bus, Take the Namsan Cable Car, Walk/Hike, or book your Seoul City Bus tickets.
