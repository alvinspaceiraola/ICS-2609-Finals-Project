Dubai

Currency

United Arab Emirates Dirham (AED) ‘د.إ’


Language

Arabic, English


Visa

Transit

  • Single entry for 4 days.

Tourist

  • Single entry for 30-90 days.
  • Multiple entry for 30-90 days.


Electric

Type C, D, and G. Standard Voltage is 220V. Standard Frequency 50 Hz.


Timezone

Gulf Standard Time (GMT+4)

Best Time to Go

The best time to go to is during the winter (October to February).


Weather

Dubai experiences 2 seasons: Summer and Winter. During October, the temperature decreases as the wind blows cooler. You will determine that the place is approaching the summer season during February as the wind and environment increases humidity.

Summer - March to September
Winter - October to February


Getting Around

  • RTA Bus

    • The city is filled with bus stops that contains a waiting room with a screen that shows how long until the next bus arrives (consistently every 10-15 mins). All you need is to apply for an RTA Nol Card (buy in every metro station nearby)

  • Taxi

    • Dubai always had taxis roaming around in almost every part of the city which makes it easier for people to find one.

  • Dubai Metro Train

    • First, you need to buy an RTA Nol Card (for AED 20 only) to be able to ride the train which goes from an end to end of the city. The train route consists of 2 lines:
    Red line : Jebel Ali to Rashidiya (vice-versa)
    Green line : Creek to Etisalat (vice-versa)


Popular Attractions in Dubai


Burj Khalifa

Tallest building in the world (828m or 2716.5 feet tall).
- The Dubai Fountain dances in front of the Burj Khalifa every 30 minutes at 6:00pm. It also holds the world’s largest dancing fountain system.
- It houses a large art collection (more than 100-pieces of art) made by the world’s best-known artists.


Dubai: Miracle Garden

See more than 45 million flowers in Dubai’s Miracle Garden that includes time to shop at the Global Village. Marvel at the eye catching colors of the world’s largest natural flower garden, including the beautiful butterfly garden.
