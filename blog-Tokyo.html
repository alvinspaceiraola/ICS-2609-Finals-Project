Electric

Japan uses two associated plug types, Plug Type A and Plug Type B.


Timezone

Japan Standard Time (GMT+9)

Best Time to Go

Spring is strikingly beautiful in Tokyo, especially when the flowers are in full bloom. Tokyo records high numbers of tourists during this time to witness the beautiful scenery of spring.


Weather

The climate of Tokyo during summers are characterized by hot, humid, and wet weather, while the winters are moderately wet and cold. Winter mostly happens during December up until March, Spring happens during March until June, Summer is from June to September, while Autumn is from September to December.


Getting Around

The main source of transportation that you would have to utilize is Tokyo’s Metro, and it may involve having to frequently switch subway lines. You would often find elementary schoolers travelling alone on their way to their destinations, so it is evident that traversing Tokyo is extremely easy. There are also some officers always willing to help you whenever you need it, so the probability of getting lost or stuck is low.


Popular Attractions in Tokyo


Tokyo Dome City Attractions and TeNQ Space Museum

Enjoy access to 4 Tokyo Dome City attractions of your choice and entry to TeNQ Space Museum where you can zip through the park at 130 km/hour with the Thunder Dolphine Roller Coaster, make a splash on the Water Drop water slide, get a panoramic view of Tokyo atop the iconic Big O ferris wheel, or even discover the wonders of the galaxy with the interactive exhibits at TeNQ Space Museum


Borderless – Odaiba

TeamLab Borderless is a digital art museum located in Odaiba, Tokyo. The museum is an audiovisual creation by TeamLab and its creative programmers, animators, mathematicians, architects, and designers.
